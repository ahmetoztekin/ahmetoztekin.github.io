About
Driven by the molecular
mysteries of the brain

I'm a second-year Molecular Biology and Genetics student at Bilkent University with a passion for understanding how cognition can be explained at the molecular level. I conduct pharmacological research under Prof. Dr. Hale Tufan at TOBB ETU Medical School and am currently working on a systematic review and meta-analysis.

I previously contributed as a freelance science writer for Evrim Ağacı, one of Turkey's largest science communities, and I currently serve as a board member of the Bilkent Interdisciplinary Biology Society (InterBio), bridging research and public outreach.

Beyond the lab, I enjoy designing websites, singing countertenor opera, and exploring jazz music. I'm driven by a lifelong curiosity about neuroplasticity, the molecular underpinnings of cognition, and the mechanisms behind neurodegenerative diseases.
